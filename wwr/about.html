Rapids-Raft

Our purpose at White Water Expeditions is to merge the thrill of high-adventure rafting with unwavering commitment to river conservation. We strive to be the premier guide service, fostering deep respect for nature and creating unforgettable, safe experiences that empower our clients. Our creed is simple: **Safety first, stewardship always, and adventure without compromise.

History

Founded in the spring of 1998 by a group of passionate former park rangers, White Water Expeditions began with a single, battered raft named 'The Voyager' and a mission to explore the wildest stretches of the Salmon River. Over the next two decades, we expanded from a grassroots operation into a certified outfitter, pioneering new safety techniques and leading expeditions across five major Western river systems. While much has changed, our dedication to conservation and the pure joy of navigating challenging rapids remains the core of our legacy.
